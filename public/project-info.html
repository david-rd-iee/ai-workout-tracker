Project Description

The AI Workout Tracker is a comprehensive cross-platform application designed to streamline the management of fitness training sessions between personal trainers and their clients. The application features real-time communication capabilities, allowing trainers and clients to interact seamlessly through instant messaging and group chats. Clients can easily book training sessions based on trainer availability, with integrated calendar support to manage schedules effectively.

Key Features

  • Real-time Communication: Instant messaging between trainers and clients with group chat support
  • Session Management: Booking system with availability scheduling and calendar integration
  • AI Workout Assistant: Intelligent chatbot providing personalized exercise recommendations
  • Leaderboards: Competitive rankings to encourage user engagement and motivation
  • Achievement System: Badges and milestones to gamify the fitness experience
  • Payment Processing: Integrated payment handling for trainer services
  • Session Notes: Trainers can maintain detailed client progress records
  • Cross-Platform: Native experience on iOS, Android, and web browsers
  • Offline Capability: Core features accessible without internet connection
